// 2.15.25 //

〚AFTERLOVE EP〛

After non-stop work on coding and at the café, it was finally time to take a break with some laid back gaming! Today we tried out AfterLove EP, a new indie game released yesterday by Pikselnesia, with art by soyatu! The game follows Rama, a musician in a band with dreams of making it big. However, his girlfriend and biggest muse, Cinta, suddenly passes away, and Rama ghosts his bandmates and the world around him. The game takes place in Jakarta, so the setting was really unique! Be sure to check it out if you're interested~! We had some banter between scenes, especially about having mutual trust between myself and you seeder! Remember... if you don't trust me you might get turned into sausages!

〚404 CAFE SPECIAL〛

Happy Valentine's Day~! We celebrated 404 Café style with pour-over coffee and homemade chocolates! It was my first time attempting to make my own chocolates! I was about as ponkotsu as expected, and made a huge mess in the process of filling donut-shaped silicon molds with melted chocolate, as well as dipping some of my favorite Moomin buscuits in what was left! While letting them chill in the freezer, I sang some love songs for karaoke~! As I wrapped up the chocolates in a cute tenshi-style box for seeder, the stream was wrapped up as well!
